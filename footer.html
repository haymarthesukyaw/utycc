About YCC

University of Technology (Yatanarpon Cyber City) is one of the Centers of Excellence (COE) in Myanmar and one of their visions is to train skillful and proficient engineers and in order to fulfill this vision, they desire support and technical transfer from organizations from more developed countries. The main reason why UTYCC is interested in the USAID grant is because the activities of readiness of Myanmar teaching staff, engineers and skilled workers in AFTA and in the region. UTYCC will integrate with the participating departments to provide MekongSkills2Work training program not only to local staff, but also to other institutions, organizations and agencies.
